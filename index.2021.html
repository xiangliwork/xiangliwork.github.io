Artisan's Workshop is a product with my passion and social values. It's my second indie app, from design to publish all by myself. I worked on Artisan's Workshop mainly in 2014.

I'm very passionate on learning, because I want to be better and better, that brings happiness to people I care. I volunteered to guide high school students in mine and my wife's hometowns a few times in 2013&2014. After talking to them many times, I found two major problems. Some of them have no idea why they should spend time on learning, others just want to get high score in NCEE(National College Entrance Examination) but they couldn't do it well even though they spent enough time. To help them solve those problems, I created Artisan's Workshop.

I hope young people open their minds and think independently by reading what my friends and I share to them, then achieve their goals by actual practice. These features are all in this app. It contains reading, a built-in PDCA(planâ€“doâ€“checkâ€“adjust) cycle, reminders, statistics, notes, etc..

BB Reminder - remind people you care
Chinese Only. Chinese Name: bbæé†’
last update: April 2014

BB Reminder is a product full of care and love. It's my first indie app, from design to publish all by myself. I worked on BB Reminder mainly in 2013.

My wife was having pills every two days back then, she always couldn't remember it right. I should help, right? I tried numbers of apps, none of them could fullfil my requirement. Then I created BB Reminder, supporting customized repeat intervals and easy to inform other people via WeChat/Message/PhoneCall.

I was surprisingly happy that BB Reminder got its funs! I received a lot of feedbacks, then I kept it up, delivered 10 distinctive features in 7 updates in total, those features made BB Reminder so different from other reminder apps that 3 VCs contacted me to talk about potential coorporation. Unfortunately, my MBP was stolen in April, 2014, I was so fool that all my codes were on the local git storage, and I didn't use Time Machine either. BB Reminder stopped updates since then. I always push my codes and docs to Github and use Time Machine frequently ever after.
